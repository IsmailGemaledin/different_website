Our system leverages advanced computer vision and artificial intelligence to analyze apples in real time. Using a camera and custom-trained models, we identify apple varieties, assess their quality, and detect imperfections that may not be visible to the naked eye. The process is designed to be fast, accurate, and accessible for everyone, regardless of their background or abilities.

The user simply places an apple in front of the camera. The system then scans the fruit, determines its type, evaluates its condition, and provides instant feedback. This information is delivered in a clear, user-friendly format, including audio guidance for those who need it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the system accessible for people with visual impairments?

Yes! The system provides clear audio feedback and intuitive visual cues, making it easy for everyone to use, including those with visual impairments.

Can the system help people with specific health conditions?

Absolutely. The system offers personalized recommendations, such as identifying apples suitable for people with GERD and provides nutritional information for each variety.

What do I need to use this system?

The setup is simple and user-friendly, with step-by-step guidance provided.

Can the system be used for other fruits?

Currently, the system is optimized for apples, but we are working on expanding support to other fruits in the future.
